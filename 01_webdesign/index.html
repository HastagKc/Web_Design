Lion

The lion, Panthera leo, is a majestic and social big cat found in various parts of Africa and a small population in India. Living in prides, they exhibit distinctive features, with males sporting impressive manes. Lions are known for their powerful roars and cooperative hunting behavior. Primarily carnivorous, their diet includes large ungulates. Facing conservation challenges, efforts are essential to protect these iconic creatures from threats such as habitat loss and human-wildlife conflict.
